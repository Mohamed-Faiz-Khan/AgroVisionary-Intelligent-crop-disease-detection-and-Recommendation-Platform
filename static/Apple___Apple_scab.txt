Apple Scab Management
1. Choose Resistant Varieties: Select resistant apple varieties like 'Liberty' or 'Enterprise.'
2. Sanitation: Remove fallen leaves and fruit to reduce fungal spores.
3. Fungicide Sprays: Apply preventive fungicides (e.g., chlorothalonil) during flowering and new leaf emergence.
4. Improve Air Circulation: Prune trees for better airflow.
5. Avoid Overhead Watering: Use drip irrigation to reduce leaf wetness.
6. Monitor for Symptoms: Regularly check for dark spots on leaves and monitor weather conditions.
